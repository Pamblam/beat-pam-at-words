Beat Pam at Words with Friends

I made this so I beat Pam at Words with Friends. It's easier to write a bot than to learn new words.

Usage:
  1. Add the letters you have available.
  2. Each time Pam makes a move, add the word, the row and column at which it stars, and it's direction, and update the board. Do the same whenever you make a move, so the board below reflects the game board.
  3. When it is your turn, use the button to find the best possible move given your current letters and the letters on the board. After you make your move, dont forget to update your letters.
